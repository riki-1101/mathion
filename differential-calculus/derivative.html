導関数

導関数の定義

導関数 \(f'(x)\) は \(y=f(x)\) のとき

\[ y',\quad \frac{dy}{dx},\quad \frac{d}{dx}f(x),\quad \frac{df}{dx}(x),\quad \{f(x)\}' \]

などとも書かれます。

導関数の性質

証明
  1. \( \begin{align} &\{kf(x)+lg(x)\}'\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\{kf(x+h)+lg(x+h)\}-\{kf(x)+lg(x)\}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\left\{k\cdot\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}+l\cdot\frac{g(x+h)-g(x)}{h}\right\}\\ &=kf'(x)+lg'(x) \end{align} \)
  2. \( \begin{align} &\{f(x)g(x)\}'\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{kf(x+h)g(x+h)-kf(x)g(x)}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\{f(x+h)g(x+h)-f(x)g(x+h)\}+\{f(x)g(x+h)-f(x)g(x)\}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\left\{\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\cdot g(x+h)+f(x)\cdot\frac{g(x+h)-g(x)}{h}\right\}\\ &=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x) \end{align} \)
  3. \( \begin{align} &\displaystyle\left\{\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right\}'\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\left\{\frac{f(x+h)}{g(x+h)}-\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right\}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\cdot\frac{f(x+h)g(x)-f(x)g(x+h)}{g(x+h)g(x)}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{g(x+h)g(x)}\cdot\frac{\{f(x+h)g(x)-f(x)g(x)\}-\{f(x)g(x+h)-f(x)g(x)\}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{g(x+h)g(x)}\left\{\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\cdot g(x)-f(x)\cdot\frac{g(x+h)-g(x)}{h}\right\}\\ &=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{\{g(x)\}^2} \end{align} \)

\[ \begin{align} f'(x)&=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\log\{\sin(x+h)\}-\log(\sin x)}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\log(\sin x\cos h+\cos x\sin h)-\log(\sin x)}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\cdot\log\left(\cos h+\frac{\sin h}{\tan x}\right)\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\log\left(\cos h+\frac{\sin h}{\tan x}\right)^{\frac{1}{h}}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\log\left\{\cos h\left(1+\frac{\tan h}{\tan x}\right)\right\}^{\frac{1}{h}}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\left[\log(\cos h)^\frac{1}{h}+\log\left(1+\frac{\tan h}{\tan x}\right)^{\frac{1}{h}}\right]\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\left[\log(\cos h)^\frac{1}{h}+\log\left\{\left(1+\frac{\tan h}{\tan x}\right)^{\frac{\tan x}{\tan h}}\right\}^{\frac{\tan h}{h}\frac{1}{\tan x}}\right]\\ &=\log(1)+\log(e)^{1\cdot\frac{1}{\tan x}}\\ &=\frac{1}{\tan x} \end{align} \]
